Plastic Recycling

What is Plastic Recycling?

Plastic recycling is the process of recovering waste plastic and reprocessing it into useful products. It helps reduce pollution and conserves resources.

How Can Plastic Be Recycled?

  • Sorting plastics by type.
  • Cleaning and shredding.
  • Melting and reshaping into new products.
  • Reusing plastic to create new items.

Why Recycling Matters?

Recycling plastic reduces landfill waste, saves energy, and helps protect the environment from pollution.
