Factory Waste Management

What is Industrial Waste?

Industrial waste includes materials discarded from factories, such as chemicals, metals, plastics, and gases. Managing these wastes reduces pollution and promotes sustainability.

How Factories Manage Waste?

  • Sorting waste materials.
  • Recycling metals, plastics, and paper.
  • Proper disposal of hazardous chemicals.
  • Utilizing waste-to-energy solutions.

Why Proper Waste Management Matters?

Proper waste management is important because it keeps our environment clean and safe. If trash is not thrown away properly, it can pollute the air, water, and land, harming animals and people. For example, plastic bottles thrown in rivers can hurt fish and make the water dirty. By using trash bins, recycling, and composting food waste, we can keep our surroundings healthy and beautiful.
